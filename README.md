# 🚀 Overview

DemandIQ is an AI-powered Retail Business Intelligence platform designed to help businesses transform raw retail data into meaningful business insights.

The platform combines machine learning, demand forecasting, inventory intelligence, interactive dashboards, and AI-powered business analysis into a single application.

Unlike traditional dashboards, DemandIQ not only visualizes data but also predicts future demand, identifies inventory risks, and generates executive-ready business reports.
